Boiling

Baking

Step 1

Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast in a bowl

Step 2

Add All Purpose Flour, Salt, Continue Mixing until the dough is gathered together

Step 3

Transfer to lightly floured counter and knead until dough is smooth

Step 4

Form into a ball and place in an oiled ball

Step 5

Release the dough by punching out the dough

Step 6

Release the dough by punching out the dough

Step 7

Take each dough ball and poke a hole, widen as shown

Step 8

Bring a large pot of water with sugar to boil, boil each side for 25 sec for each bagel

Step 9

Drain the excess water and place in baking sheet

Step 10

Bake in a preheated oven at 410F/210C for 20 minutes
